Description

Discover the ultimate gaming innovation with Arbiter Studio. Our keyboards feature Hall Effect Magnetic Switches and Rapid Trigger technology. This limited edition Polar 65 keyboard comes with exclusive next-gen Dual-Rail Fuji Switches, crafted for peak performance and buttery typing sound.

In collaboration with Nacho Customz, featuring his signature design, the Calabera Edition is created. Debuting the artwork together on Arbiter's new custom PBT keycaps, the New AOP profile features wider canvas for dye-sub design and cherry-like curvature for gaming comfort.

Features

  • Fuji Dual-Rail Magnetic Switches - 36g Linear
  • Adjustable Actuation from 0.1mm - 3.8mm
  • Rapid Trigger
  • Custom 5-Sided Dye Sub PBT Keycaps (AOP)
  • CNC Aluminum Frame
  • PCB Mounted with Screw-in Stabilizer (Spacebar 6.25u)
  • Ultra Bright RGB Lighting with 16 modes
  • Web App Compatible
  • Limited Edition
  • Keyboard Weight: 2.2lbs or 2030g

Specifications

  • FORM FACTOR: 65% (Number of Keys 68)
  • LAYOUT: ANSI (US) Layout
  • KEY SWITCHES: Fuji Dual Rail - 36g Linear Hall Effect Magnetic Switches Hot swappable
  • BACKLIGHT: RGB - 16 Modes of Preset Lighting Effect
  • CONNECTIVITY: Detachable Wired USB 2.0 (USB-C to A)
  • FRAME: CNC Aluminum
  • DAMPENING: PCBA Silicone, Bottom Case Silicone
  • KEYCAP: AOP Keycap (Arbiter Original Profile) PBT Material with Dye-Sub Artwork 1.5mm Thick Walls
  • MOUNTING PLATE: Anodize Aluminum
  • STABILIZER: 1x PCB Mounted with Screw-in Stabilizer (Spacebar 6.25u) 3x Plate Mounted Stabilizer (Shift, Back Space, Enter 2u)
  • POLLING RATE: 1000 Hz
  • OS COMPATIBILITY: Windows® 11, 10
  • WEIGHT: 2.2lbs or 2030g (KEYBOARD ONLY)
  • CABLE: 1.8M Braided USB
